Aerial

Size: 8” x 11” and 11” x 17” photo prints

Media: photography, video

Materials: paper, computer, video projector



3-14-14 Davis, California. This project is composed of photos and video footage taken in a geographical examination of Davis, uncovering and re-presenting nearby sights and sounds in a deliberately subjective way. Footage ranges from aerial shots of very high to very low shots from the ground. In the video chronicle, a street view is contrasted with an ant’s perspective on a Davis garden. Exhibited in the show DAVIS SPACE, Davis Makerspace.


pilot: Jim Stewart
