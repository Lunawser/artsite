PROFESSIONAL & ACADEMIC PROFILE



  • Graphic design and Web-design at Eyebeam Art and Technology Center and The Wardrobe, website and event signage. See my design portfolio.
  • University instructor in 2-dimensional experimental animation course.
  • Film Screening Curator at Oddball Film and Video Archive.
  • Interactive art installation exhibitions in Marian Spore Art Museum and 58 N. 6th Media Labs in New York.
  • Extensive collaboration experience in graduate-level, grant-funded projects such as the Chashama Windows Performance spaces.
  • An academic focus on media theory, art, and culture; experience presenting at international conferences such as Inter-Disciplinary.Net’s Knowledge, Power, & Intellectuals and Trondheim Electronic Art Center’s Making Reality Real.
  • Experience overseeing assistants and coordinating large-scale departmental equipment organization for PIMA at Brooklyn College, CUNY.
  • Community leadership and committee work in a student-run / consensus-based program in University of Washington’s CEP.
  • Expediency with administrative work such as writing reports, and creating contract lists and reviewing literary submissions at Curtis Brown, Ltd. in New York.
  • Serving on the Board of Directors at Davis Makerspace in Davis, CA.
