Cycle Goggles

Size: 38” x 14” x 44”

Media: sculpture, video

Materials: wood, plastic, digital picture frame, acrylic paints, magnifying glass

 

5-6-11 Davis, California. Cyclists have an all-consuming relationship with weather patterns. This project tries to go literally inside head of one cyclist captured in a sporty specimen box. Note the "goggles" (magnifying glass, aluminum tubes, and a digital picture frame) that make this all possible. This sculpture was shown at Art Gallery in the Davis, CA Whole Earth Festival.
