heart search

Size: 16” x 8” x 8”

Media: sculpture, video, mixed media

Materials: paper mache, acrylic paints, glass jar, iPod



11-7-09 Brooklyn, New York. A tiny heart-shaped sculpture has a square opening that shows video of a spacious and dark interior. Like a magical suitcase with endless room, the optical illusion presents as as a conundrum, both physically and metaphorically. Exhibited during Play With Fire Video Arts Festival, Troutman Studios, Brooklyn, NY.
