Melting Commons

 

This video is a meditation on water use in suburban California. Here we see two totem animals of modern times reading books to each other: one reads a book on sport tactics and the other, some juicy scenes from a romance novel. Are they jays, blackbirds, or crows? Hard to tell, but they read and gesture at each other in a modular human kitchen as beautiful but sinister drops of water, ice, ripples of lakes, and oceans waves interrupt them.
