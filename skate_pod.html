"Check out my insides" -- Lilo the skatepod

 

This animation is an imaginary exploration of a wrongly identified "piece of seaweed" found on the beach. It turned out to be the pod of an animal similar to a sting-ray; here, the pod (now named "Lilo") reveals it's insides and grows into an adult (October 2010).
