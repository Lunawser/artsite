THINK THINK THINK WHY WHY WHY

Media: performance

Artists: Emily Schleiner, Jean Ann Douglass, Kristen Tretheway



6-3-09 New York, New York. Art project THINK THINK THINK WHY WHY WHY began when three people decided to start a religion. Rather than follow already existing traditions, the group used a more democratic and do-it-yourself philosophy to structure their practice and belief. Drawing anonymously turned in "daily tasks" out of a hat, the group invited passersby to join them at their Welcome Center on 266 West 37th St, a Chachama Windows performance space awarded to the group. They hosted multiple events and workshops, including screening their exercise video. [See below]
